Creators

The Panama Pacific Exposition Company was held to the task of assembling a team of the best architects, designers, artists, and engineers from around the country to create a design worthy of a grand presentation of artistry and achievement. A big hurdle for the designers of the Exposition was the challenge to create a cohesive design that would bring together the various structures and exhibits under an underlying idea. The Exposition had to accomodate monumental structures, diverse exhibitions, and thousands of visitors; it had to be efficient and theatrical; and it had to complement the natural beauty of the San Francisco Bay.
