Bibliography for this Site

A number of scholars have helped this project as well as make publically available the hard to find images and texts in reference to the Exposition. Their vast knowledge of the PPIE helped generate the written portion of this project.

  • Moore, Sarah J. Empire On Display : San Francisco's Panama-Pacific International Exposition Of 1915. n.p.: Norman : University of Oklahoma Press, [2013], 2013. Ignacio: USF Libraries Catalog.
  • Ackley, Laura A. San Francisco's Jewel City : The Panama-Pacific International Exposition Of 1915. n.p.: Berkeley : Heyday ; San Francisco : California Historical Society, [2014], 2014. Ignacio: USF Libraries Catalog.
  • Abigail, Markwyn. Empress San Francisco : The Pacific Rim, The Great West, And California At The Panama-Pacific International Exposition. Lincoln: University of Nebraska Press, 2014. Project MUSE.
  • Panama-Pacific International Exposition (1915 : San Francisco, Calif.). Official Guide Of The Panama-Pacific International Exposition, 1915 : San Francisco, California, U.S.A. : Opening Day, February 20, 1915, Closing Day, December 4, 1915. n.p.: Wahlgreen Co., 1915. Center for Research Libraries.
